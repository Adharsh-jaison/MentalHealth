Welcome to Your MCS Center

Welcome to the Mental Health Community System at Christ (Deemed to be University) Lavasa Campus! Our community is committed to providing a nurturing space for individuals to share experiences, seek support, and access resources for mental well-being.

Through peer engagement, counseling services, and collaborative initiatives, we strive to create an inclusive and empathetic environment, fostering a culture that prioritizes mental health within the Christ University Lavasa community.
